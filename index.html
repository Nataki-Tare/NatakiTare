About Us

“Nataki Tare” is a organisation which is slowly becoming purpose of our lives. “Nataki Tare” is becoming home of creativity. we have future of film industry with us. we have film directors, writers, cinematographers, music directors, singers, editors, marketing team in our team. yes ! we believe we have incredible creative powers and efficient execution strength. We believe in concept of “Kalpavriksh” ! We are here to make content that really breathes cinema. we are certainly very clear about that and “no absurdity” is what we believe in. this is our mantra and we will never compromise on it at any cost. We have grown from grass root, performing street plays, theatre, college plays etc. we value your participation in helping us make a permanent home for artist where they can earn feel safe.
